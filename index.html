Setify
NodeJS | ReactJS

Setify is a tool that allows you to search for an artist, see their history of concerts, and save any of their setlists played as a Spotify playlist. Created initially as a way to learn React, and because it was something I wanted to use, I recently developed a new iteration.
It's been added to the Spotify Developer Showcase which is very cool.
